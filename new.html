ABOUT ME

I am Leela Krishna Vemulapalli, a second-year mechanical engineering student with a strong passion for drone technology, robotics, and simulations. I'm eager to improve my technical skills and dive deeper into ROS 2, PX4, GAZEBO and UAV development through research and internships.

Currently serving as the Vice-President of YugTronix, our university's first robotics club, I lead a team developing quadcopters and other autonomous systems. My ultimate goal is to work on autonomous drones and bring innovative ideas to life in this field.

I believe in the power of continuous learning and innovation to solve real-world problems through engineering excellence.
